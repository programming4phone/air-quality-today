Air Quality Today
Local air quality levels...for your health.

Air Quality Today is an Angular 2 application that accesses air quality measurements directly from the Environmental Protection Agency.

Just enter your 5-digit zip code to retrieve the most recent air quality reading for your location. A map is displayed showing the location of the closest observation station.

By knowing local air quality levels, you can take precautions to protect you and your family. Exposure to increased levels of air pollution can aggravate existing respiratory conditions and cause unwanted health problems.
